Nottingham Database

This is a collection of 1200 British and American folk tunes, (hornpipe, jigs, and etc.) that was created by Eric Foxley and posted on Eric Foxeley's Music Database. The database was converted to abc music notation format and was posted on abc.sourceforge.net. The collection was edited by myself correcting missing beats during repeats and transitions, and adding links to YouTube videos (still ongoing). The latest versions are available here.
